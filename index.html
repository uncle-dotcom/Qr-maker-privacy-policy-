Privacy Policy for QR Maker Pro

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

QR Maker Pro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles your information.

Information Collection

QR Maker Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The app does not require:

  • User accounts
  • Registration
  • Login
  • Personal information

Internet Connection

QR Maker Pro works completely offline. An internet connection is not required to generate QR codes.

If you create a QR code containing a website URL, an internet connection may be required when someone scans the QR code and opens the website.

Data Storage

Generated QR codes and QR code history are stored locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device unless you choose to share a QR code.

Data Sharing

QR Maker Pro does not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

Permissions

QR Maker Pro only requests the permissions necessary for saving and sharing QR codes. No unnecessary permissions are requested.

Children's Privacy

QR Maker Pro does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
